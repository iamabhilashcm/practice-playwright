2. What is auto-waiting in Playwright?
    Playwright waits for elements to be actionable before performing actions.
    “Auto-waiting reduces explicit waits and makes tests more stable by ensuring elements are ready before interaction.”

3. What are locators in Playwright?
In Playwright, locators are the way to find and interact with elements on a web page.
    EX; buttons, input fields, links, text, forms
